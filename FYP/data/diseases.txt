=== CARDIOLOGY & HEART SURGERY ===
(Covers: Cardiologist, Cardiac Surgeon, Interventional Cardiologist, Cardiovascular Surgeon, Thoracic Surgeon)

DISEASE: CORONARY ARTERY DISEASE (CAD)
SYMPTOMS: Chest pain (angina), shortness of breath, fatigue, heart attack signs (pain in arm/jaw).
CAUSES: Plaque buildup (cholesterol), smoking, hypertension, diabetes, sedentary lifestyle.
TREATMENT: Statins, aspirin, beta-blockers, angioplasty (stents), bypass surgery (CABG).

DISEASE: HYPERTENSION (HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE)
SYMPTOMS: Often silent; severe cases cause headaches, nosebleeds, vision problems, chest pounding.
CAUSES: High salt diet, obesity, stress, genetics, kidney disease.
TREATMENT: ACE inhibitors, diuretics, lifestyle changes (low sodium diet, exercise).

DISEASE: ARRHYTHMIA (IRREGULAR HEARTBEAT)
SYMPTOMS: Palpitations (fluttering chest), dizziness, fainting, shortness of breath.
CAUSES: Heart tissue scarring, electrolyte imbalance, stress, caffeine/alcohol.
TREATMENT: Anti-arrhythmic drugs, pacemaker, cardioversion, catheter ablation.

=== DERMATOLOGY & COSMETOLOGY ===
(Covers: Dermatologist, Cosmetologist, Aesthetic Physician, Hair Transplant Surgeon, Laser Specialist)

DISEASE: ACNE VULGARIS
SYMPTOMS: Blackheads, whiteheads, papules, pustules, painful cysts, scarring.
CAUSES: Excess oil (sebum), clogged pores, bacteria (C. acnes), hormonal fluctuations.
TREATMENT: Topical retinoids, benzoyl peroxide, antibiotics (doxycycline), chemical peels, laser therapy.

DISEASE: ECZEMA (ATOPIC DERMATITIS)
SYMPTOMS: Dry, itchy skin, red patches (hands, feet, neck), cracked or scaly skin.
CAUSES: Immune system overreaction, genetics, environmental triggers (soaps, stress).
TREATMENT: Moisturizers, corticosteroid creams, antihistamines, phototherapy.

DISEASE: PSORIASIS
SYMPTOMS: Red patches of skin covered with thick, silvery scales, dry/cracked skin, itching.
CAUSES: Autoimmune disease speeding up skin cell growth cycle.
TREATMENT: Topical corticosteroids, vitamin D analogues, biologics, light therapy.

DISEASE: ALOPECIA (HAIR LOSS)
SYMPTOMS: Gradual thinning on top of head, circular or patchy bald spots, sudden loosening of hair.
CAUSES: Genetics (male/female pattern baldness), stress, hormonal changes, medical conditions.
TREATMENT: Minoxidil, Finasteride, hair transplant surgery, PRP therapy.

=== DENTISTRY & ORAL SURGERY ===
(Covers: Dentist, Orthodontist, Periodontist, Implantologist, Endodontist, Oral Surgeon)

DISEASE: DENTAL CARIES (CAVITIES)
SYMPTOMS: Toothache, sensitivity to hot/cold, visible holes in teeth, pain when biting.
CAUSES: Bacteria converting sugar/starch into acid that destroys enamel.
TREATMENT: Fluoride, fillings, crowns, root canals (if nerve is infected).

DISEASE: PERIODONTITIS (GUM DISEASE)
SYMPTOMS: Swollen gums, bleeding while brushing, receding gums, loose teeth, bad breath.
CAUSES: Poor oral hygiene leading to plaque and tartar buildup.
TREATMENT: Scaling and root planing (deep cleaning), gum graft surgery, antibiotics.

DISEASE: MALOCCLUSION (MISALIGNED TEETH)
SYMPTOMS: Overbite, underbite, difficulty chewing, speech impediments, jaw pain.
CAUSES: Genetics, thumb sucking in childhood, tooth loss.
TREATMENT: Braces, clear aligners (Invisalign), jaw surgery (orthognathic surgery).

=== NEUROLOGY & NEUROSURGERY ===
(Covers: Neurologist, Neurosurgeon, Spinal Surgeon, Pediatric Neurologist)

DISEASE: EPILEPSY
SYMPTOMS: Seizures (uncontrollable jerking), temporary confusion, staring spells, loss of consciousness.
CAUSES: Genetic influence, head trauma, brain conditions (stroke/tumor), infectious diseases.
TREATMENT: Anti-epileptic drugs (AEDs), ketogenic diet, vagus nerve stimulation, brain surgery.

DISEASE: MIGRAINE
SYMPTOMS: Severe throbbing pain (often one-sided), nausea, sensitivity to light/sound, visual aura.
CAUSES: Hormonal changes, stress, sensory stimuli, sleep changes, food triggers.
TREATMENT: Triptans, pain relievers, anti-nausea meds, preventive medications (beta-blockers).

DISEASE: HERNIATED DISC (SLIPPED DISC)
SYMPTOMS: Arm or leg pain, numbness or tingling, weakness in muscles served by affected nerves.
CAUSES: Wear and tear (disk degeneration), strain from lifting, injury.
TREATMENT: Physical therapy, muscle relaxants, cortisone injections, discectomy surgery.

=== ORTHOPEDICS, PHYSIOTHERAPY & RHEUMATOLOGY ===
(Covers: Orthopedic Surgeon, Rheumatologist, Physiotherapist, Chiropractor, Sports Medicine)

DISEASE: OSTEOARTHRITIS
SYMPTOMS: Joint pain, stiffness, tenderness, loss of flexibility, grating sensation.
CAUSES: Aging, joint injury, obesity (stress on weight-bearing joints).
TREATMENT: NSAIDs (painkillers), physical therapy, corticosteroid injections, joint replacement.

DISEASE: RHEUMATOID ARTHRITIS
SYMPTOMS: Tender/warm/swollen joints, joint stiffness in mornings, fatigue, fever.
CAUSES: Autoimmune disorder attacking joint lining (synovium).
TREATMENT: DMARDs (methotrexate), biologics, physical therapy.

DISEASE: SCIATICA
SYMPTOMS: Pain radiating from lower back down the leg, burning sensation, numbness.
CAUSES: Herniated disc or bone spur compressing the sciatic nerve.
TREATMENT: Chiropractic adjustment, physical therapy, cold/hot packs, anti-inflammatories.

=== GASTROENTEROLOGY & HEPATOLOGY ===
(Covers: Gastroenterologist, Hepatologist, Hepatobiliary Surgeon, Colorectal Surgeon)

DISEASE: GERD (ACID REFLUX)
SYMPTOMS: Heartburn, chest pain, difficulty swallowing, regurgitation of food/sour liquid.
CAUSES: Weak lower esophageal sphincter, obesity, hiatal hernia.
TREATMENT: Antacids, proton pump inhibitors (PPIs), lifestyle changes (avoid triggers).

DISEASE: HEPATITIS (B & C)
SYMPTOMS: Fatigue, flu-like symptoms, dark urine, pale stool, abdominal pain, jaundice (yellow skin).
CAUSES: Viral infection (blood/body fluids), alcohol abuse (for alcoholic hepatitis).
TREATMENT: Antiviral medications, interferon injections, liver transplant (severe cases).

DISEASE: HEMORRHOIDS (PILES)
SYMPTOMS: Itching/irritation in anal region, pain, swelling, bleeding during bowel movements.
CAUSES: Straining during bowel movements, chronic constipation, pregnancy.
TREATMENT: High fiber diet, topical creams, rubber band ligation, hemorrhoidectomy.

=== ENDOCRINOLOGY & DIABETES ===
(Covers: Endocrinologist, Diabetologist, Thyroid Surgeon, Diabetes Counsellor)

DISEASE: TYPE 2 DIABETES
SYMPTOMS: Increased thirst, frequent urination, extreme hunger, fatigue, blurred vision, slow healing.
CAUSES: Insulin resistance, obesity, inactivity, genetics.
TREATMENT: Metformin, insulin therapy, diet/exercise management, bariatric surgery.

DISEASE: HYPOTHYROIDISM
SYMPTOMS: Fatigue, cold sensitivity, constipation, dry skin, weight gain, puffy face.
CAUSES: Hashimoto's disease (autoimmune), thyroid surgery, radiation therapy.
TREATMENT: Synthetic thyroid hormone (Levothyroxine).

DISEASE: HYPERTHYROIDISM
SYMPTOMS: Weight loss, rapid heartbeat, sweating, nervousness, irritability.
CAUSES: Graves' disease, thyroid nodules, thyroiditis.
TREATMENT: Radioactive iodine, anti-thyroid medications, thyroidectomy.

=== PULMONOLOGY, ASTHMA & ENT ===
(Covers: Pulmonologist, Asthma Specialist, ENT Specialist, ENT Surgeon)

DISEASE: ASTHMA
SYMPTOMS: Shortness of breath, chest tightness, wheezing, coughing attacks (worse at night).
CAUSES: Airborne allergens (pollen, mites), respiratory infections, physical activity, cold air.
TREATMENT: Rescue inhalers (Albuterol), inhaled corticosteroids, leukotriene modifiers.

DISEASE: CHRONIC OBSTRUCTIVE PULMONARY DISEASE (COPD)
SYMPTOMS: Chronic cough (smoker's cough), mucus production, shortness of breath, wheezing.
CAUSES: Long-term exposure to irritating gases (cigarette smoke), pollution.
TREATMENT: Bronchodilators, inhaled steroids, oxygen therapy, pulmonary rehabilitation.

DISEASE: SINUSITIS (CHRONIC)
SYMPTOMS: Nasal inflammation, thick discharge, congestion, pain around eyes/cheeks, reduced smell.
CAUSES: Nasal polyps, deviated septum, respiratory infections, allergies.
TREATMENT: Saline irrigation, nasal corticosteroids, antibiotics (if bacterial), sinus surgery.

=== UROLOGY & NEPHROLOGY ===
(Covers: Urologist, Nephrologist, Andrologist, Renal Surgeon, Kidney Transplant Surgeon)

DISEASE: KIDNEY STONES
SYMPTOMS: Severe sharp pain in side/back, pain on urination, pink/red/brown urine, nausea.
CAUSES: Dehydration, high-protein/salt/sugar diets, obesity.
TREATMENT: Drinking water, pain relievers, alpha blockers, shock wave lithotripsy.

DISEASE: CHRONIC KIDNEY DISEASE (CKD)
SYMPTOMS: Nausea, vomiting, loss of appetite, fatigue, sleep issues, swelling of feet/ankles.
CAUSES: Type 1 or 2 diabetes, high blood pressure, glomerulonephritis.
TREATMENT: Controlling BP/sugar, dialysis, kidney transplant.

DISEASE: ERECTILE DYSFUNCTION (ED)
SYMPTOMS: Trouble getting or keeping an erection, reduced sexual desire.
CAUSES: Heart disease, diabetes, obesity, low testosterone, stress/anxiety.
TREATMENT: Oral medications (Sildenafil), testosterone replacement, psychological counseling.

=== GYNECOLOGY, OBSTETRICS & FERTILITY ===
(Covers: Gynecologist, Obstetrician, Fertility Consultant, Maternal Fetal Medicine)

DISEASE: POLYCYSTIC OVARY SYNDROME (PCOS)
SYMPTOMS: Irregular periods, excess androgen (facial hair/acne), polycystic ovaries, infertility.
CAUSES: Excess insulin, low-grade inflammation, heredity.
TREATMENT: Birth control pills, Metformin, Clomiphene (for ovulation), lifestyle changes.

DISEASE: ENDOMETRIOSIS
SYMPTOMS: Painful periods (dysmenorrhea), pain with intercourse, pain with bowel movements, infertility.
CAUSES: Retrograde menstruation, transformation of peritoneal cells.
TREATMENT: Pain medication, hormone therapy, conservative surgery, hysterectomy.

=== PEDIATRICS ===
(Covers: Pediatrician, Neonatologist, Pediatric Surgeon, Pediatric Cardiologist, etc.)

DISEASE: OTITIS MEDIA (EAR INFECTION)
SYMPTOMS: Ear pain, tugging at ear, trouble sleeping, crying more than usual, fever.
CAUSES: Bacteria/virus in middle ear, often after cold/flu. Eustachian tube blockage.
TREATMENT: Warm compress, pain meds, antibiotics (Amoxicillin) if bacterial/severe.

DISEASE: CHICKENPOX (VARICELLA)
SYMPTOMS: Itchy rash with fluid-filled blisters, fever, loss of appetite, headache.
CAUSES: Varicella-zoster virus (highly contagious).
TREATMENT: Calamine lotion, oatmeal baths, antihistamines for itching. (Vaccine prevents it).

=== MENTAL HEALTH ===
(Covers: Psychiatrist, Psychologist, Counselor, Autism Consultant)

DISEASE: MAJOR DEPRESSIVE DISORDER
SYMPTOMS: Feelings of sadness/emptiness, loss of interest, sleep disturbances, lack of energy.
CAUSES: Biological chemistry, hormones, inherited traits, childhood trauma.
TREATMENT: Antidepressants (SSRIs), Psychotherapy (CBT), lifestyle adjustments.

DISEASE: ANXIETY DISORDER (GAD)
SYMPTOMS: Persistent worrying, overthinking, difficulty handling uncertainty, restlessness.
CAUSES: Brain chemistry, genetics, stress, personality type.
TREATMENT: Psychotherapy, anti-anxiety medications (Benzodiazepines for short term), SSRIs.

=== ONCOLOGY (CANCER) ===
(Covers: Oncologist, Radiation Oncologist, Surgical Oncologist)

DISEASE: BREAST CANCER
SYMPTOMS: Lump in breast, change in breast shape, dimpling skin, inverted nipple.
CAUSES: Hormonal, lifestyle, environmental factors. BRCA1/BRCA2 gene mutations.
TREATMENT: Lumpectomy/Mastectomy, radiation, chemotherapy, hormone therapy.

DISEASE: LUNG CANCER
SYMPTOMS: New cough that doesn't go away, coughing up blood, shortness of breath, chest pain.
CAUSES: Smoking (primary cause), secondhand smoke, radon gas, asbestos.
TREATMENT: Surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy.

=== NUTRITION & WEIGHT MANAGEMENT ===
(Covers: Nutritionist, Dietitian, Bariatric Surgeon, Obesity Specialist)

DISEASE: OBESITY
SYMPTOMS: BMI over 30, breathlessness, increased sweating, joint pain, fatigue.
CAUSES: Calorie surplus, genetics, inactivity, medications, hormonal issues.
TREATMENT: Calorie deficit diet, exercise plan, behavioral therapy, weight loss surgery.

=== EYE CARE ===
(Covers: Eye Specialist, Eye Surgeon, Optometrist, Vitreo Retina Surgeon)

DISEASE: CATARACTS
SYMPTOMS: Clouded/blurred vision, increasing difficulty with vision at night, sensitivity to light.
CAUSES: Aging, injury, diabetes, excessive UV exposure.
TREATMENT: Corrective lenses (early stages), cataract surgery (lens replacement).

DISEASE: GLAUCOMA
SYMPTOMS: Patchy blind spots (peripheral vision), tunnel vision, severe headache, eye pain.
CAUSES: High pressure in eye damaging optic nerve.
TREATMENT: Prescription eye drops, oral meds, laser treatment, surgery.
